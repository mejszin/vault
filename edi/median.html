Introducing the Median EDI client                           │                       │
~ Mejszin                                                   └───────────────────────┘

After designing an EDIFACT library and validation engine, the next logical step was
to build a nice front end. Though I want to take the project further than purely as
a validation tool. This client will be useful for multiple parts in the EDI
lifecycle such as: 

  -  piping between services with intermediate validation;
  -  archiving messages and creating a full timeline;
  -  export management (a quick replacement Amazon compliant SSCC label!);
  -  linking with client data for ease of probing user-defined values;
  -  etc.

The project is open source and available on my GitHub.
